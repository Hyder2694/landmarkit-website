About LandmarkIT

Technology hiring with a people-first mindset.

LandmarkIT is an IT staffing and software solutions company helping organizations find skilled technology professionals and build reliable software delivery teams.

Who We Are

We focus on practical, business-ready technology support. Our goal is simple: help clients find the right people and help candidates find the right opportunities.

Our Approach

  • Screen candidates for both technical fit and communication.
  • Support contract, contract-to-hire, and direct-hire needs.
  • Stay focused on long-term trust and measurable outcomes.
